About

At Foodpanda, we believe in the power of delicious food to bring joy, comfort, and connection to people's lives. We're more than just a food delivery platform; we're your trusted partner in discovering and savoring the best culinary experiences in your city. Our Story Founded in 2012, Foodpanda has grown from a small startup with a big vision into one of the world's leading online food delivery platforms. Our journey started with a simple idea: to make food delivery fast, convenient, and enjoyable for everyone. We've come a long way since then, and our commitment to this vision remains stronger than ever. Our Mission Connecting People through Food: We're on a mission to connect people, cultures, and communities through the universal language of food. Whether you're craving a comforting bowl of noodles from your favorite local restaurant or exploring exotic flavors from around the world, Foodpanda brings it all to your doorstep. Supporting Local Businesses: We're passionate about supporting local restaurants and eateries. We partner with thousands of local culinary gems, empowering them to reach a wider audience and thrive in a competitive market. Making Every Meal Special: We believe that every meal should be a special occasion. That's why we're dedicated to ensuring that your food is delivered with care and precision, so you can enjoy a delightful dining experience every time you order through Foodpanda. Our Values Customer-Centric: Your satisfaction is our top priority. We're committed to providing you with a seamless and enjoyable food ordering experience, from the moment you browse our app to the second your food arrives at your door. Innovation: We embrace innovation to continually enhance our platform, offering you the latest features, the widest selection, and the fastest delivery times. Reliability: You can count on us. We're here to deliver your favorite meals, no matter the weather, time of day, or location. We take pride in our reliability and dependability. Diversity and Inclusion: Food brings people together, and we celebrate the diversity of our customers, partners, and team members. We're dedicated to fostering an inclusive and respectful environment for all. Join Us on This Delicious Journey Foodpanda is more than a food delivery platform; it's a community of food lovers, explorers, and enthusiasts. We invite you to join us on this delicious journey as we continue to redefine the way you experience food. Thank you for choosing Foodpanda.
